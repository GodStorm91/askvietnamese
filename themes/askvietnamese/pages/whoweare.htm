"AskVietnamese - Support international visitors FOR FREE"
The idea of ASKVIETNAMESE is very simple, it is a place where any visitor can ask any question and get the answer directly from Vietnamese people. As a team working together, AskVietnamese determines to become a community to support international tourists/visitors in Vietnam. International tourists/Visitors will use AskVietnamese as a platform to find out information about: Places, Travel Tips, Safety, Events, Culture, Souvernirs. These information are confirmed to be authentic and trustworthy. Also, AskVietnamese is keen on contacting and supporting creative and cultural activities, academic research, non-profit projects, sustainable campaigns that contribute different impacts on the society
