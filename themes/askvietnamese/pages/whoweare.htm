"AskVietnamese - Support international visitors FOR FREE"
ASKVIETNAMESE - DIGITAL TOURISM MAPPING PLATFORM WITH PERSONALIZED ITINERARIES is a custom platform that provides international tourists digitalized maps with Qr code to offer them solutions for their trip. It uses structured data to capture tourist attention and tailor their experiences in order to offer them personalized itineraries. Its web-based software application and mobile app is a Q&A portal where the content (including popular sights, authentic food, cultural activities, cultural events and restaurant recommendations) is created by local community and handled by the company. We aim to apply this platform in non-native English speaking countries in Asia. Its first service which is launched in Vietnam is called “ASKVIETNAMESE”
